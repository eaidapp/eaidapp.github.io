Due to the need of monetary support by very many people in Uganda such as the charity, business people and entrepreneurs who find it hard to access such support and thus resort to posting their stories all over social media, televisions or even further move to gatherings to seek help and collect money from people, as a team of five members, we have sat and resolved to set up an application which will integrate the whole process of making donation campaigns. All people who need support will go and post their stories and users of the application will read and donate to those ones they wish through mobile money. So those looking for support end up posting their phone numbers all over social media to attract attention and enable people without cards to send them money which is susceptible to fraud, mistakes in numbers can also be made at the time of transactions. this can also delay transactions. All these can inconvinience, donors and discourage potential donors thus reducing the effectiveness and efficiency of such campaigns. The application will mainly target the charity, business people and entrepreneurs but it will be upon the donors to donate to the people they want according to their stories they have read.
