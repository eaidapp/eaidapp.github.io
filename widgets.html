eAid is a platform were verified users will be able to post their stories and start a fundraiser. Users who want to donate can tap on the story of whoever they want to help and then pass on the donation via MTN Mobile Money.

 1. Start your fundraiser

  • Set your fundraiser goal
  • Tell your story
  • Add a picture or video

 2. Share with Friends

  • Send emails
  • Send text messages
  • Share on social media

 3. Manage donations

  • Accept donations
  • Thank donors
  • Withdraw funds
