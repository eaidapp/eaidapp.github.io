eAid is a mobile application that people who need to fundraise will use to post their stories and start a fundraiser. Application users who want to donate can tap on the story of whoever they want to help and then make the donation via Mobile Money.

Sytem Requirements

Users will need a mobile android device such as smart phone to install the app.
To start a fundraiser, Users will have to create an account.
To donate, users will need a Uganda mobile money account.

Start your own fundraiser

In order to raise funds you need to install the eAid app on your smartphone and then create an account.
Set your fundraiser goal and also tell your story and add a picture about your story.

Reach out to Donors

Keep your story going by sharing it's link to supporters, friends and colleagues through email, text messages and on social media platforms.
