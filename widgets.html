eAid is a mobile application that people who need to fundraise will use to post their stories and start a fundraiser. Application users who want to donate can tap on the story of whoever they want to help and then make the donation via Mobile Money.

The following are system requirements.

>Users will need a smartphone to install the application >To start a fundraiser, Users will have to create an account. >To donate, users will need a Uganda mobile money account.

How to start your own fundraiser

In order to raise funds you need to install the eaidapp on your smartphone and then create an account. Set your fundraiser goal and also tell your story and add a picture about your story.

Reach out to donors

Keep your story going by sharing links of your page to supporters, friends and colleagues through email, text messages and on social media platforms.
